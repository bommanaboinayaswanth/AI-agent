# Company Leave Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the leave policies for all employees at TechCorp Industries.

## Types of Leave

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year.
- Leave can be carried over to the next year with a maximum of 10 days.
- Any unused leave beyond 10 days will be forfeited.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

### Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year.
- Sick leave can be used for personal illness or family medical emergencies.
- Medical certificates are required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- Unused sick leave cannot be carried over to the next year.

### Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to 4 months (120 days) of maternity leave.
- The leave begins from the date of delivery.
- Up to 1 month can be taken before the expected delivery date.
- Job protection is guaranteed during maternity leave.

### Paternity Leave
- Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paternity leave.
- Leave must be taken within 3 months of child's birth.
- Documentation of child's birth is required.

### Bereavement Leave
- Employees are entitled to 3 days of bereavement leave for immediate family members.
- Immediate family includes spouse, parents, children, and siblings.
- Additional leave can be requested on a case-by-case basis.

## Leave Request Process

1. Submit leave request via HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Obtain manager approval
3. HR will process and confirm the request
4. For extended leave, additional documentation may be required

## Policy on Leave Denial
Leave requests can be denied only in exceptional circumstances affecting critical business operations. In such cases, employees will be offered alternative dates.
