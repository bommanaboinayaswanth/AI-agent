# Employee Code of Conduct

## Purpose
This code of conduct establishes guidelines for professional behavior and ethical standards at TechCorp Industries.

## General Conduct
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain professional communication at all times
- Report violations of company policies to HR
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

## Dress Code
- Business casual is the standard dress code for office work
- Smart casual is acceptable for client meetings
- Casual attire is permitted on designated "Casual Fridays"
- Specific departments may have different requirements

## Harassment and Discrimination
- Zero tolerance policy for any form of harassment or discrimination
- This includes harassment based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, etc.
- All complaints will be investigated confidentially
- Remedial action will be taken against violators

## Confidentiality
- Employees must maintain confidentiality of company information
- Do not share proprietary information without authorization
- NDAs must be signed by all employees
- Violation may result in legal action

## Conflict of Interest
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest to your manager
- Do not accept gifts or benefits from vendors or clients
- Gifts with value above Rs. 500 must be reported to HR
- Do not engage in activities that compete with company business

## Substance Abuse
- Strict no-tolerance policy for substance abuse
- Random drug testing may be conducted
- Violation will result in immediate termination
- Rehabilitation support is available for employees seeking help

## Social Media
- Respect company confidentiality on personal social media accounts
- Do not misrepresent yourself as company representative
- Company reserves the right to take action against inappropriate posts
- Personal opinions should be clearly marked as such

## Disciplinary Action
Violations may result in:
1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Suspension
4. Termination

Severity of violation determines the level of action taken.
