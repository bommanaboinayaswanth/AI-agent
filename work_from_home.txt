# Work From Home Policy

## Eligibility
All full-time employees are eligible to work from home (WFH) after successfully completing their probation period (minimum 3 months).

## WFH Days
- Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week
- WFH schedule must be coordinated with the team and manager
- At least 2 days per week must be spent in the office for collaboration

## Equipment and Setup
- Company will provide a laptop and monitor for remote work
- Employees are responsible for maintaining a productive work environment
- Internet speed must be at least 10 Mbps for optimal performance
- VPN access will be provided by IT for secure connectivity

## Working Hours
- Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST
- Employees can set their own start/end time within 8 AM to 6 PM window
- All employees must be available during core hours for meetings

## Responsibilities
- Maintain data security and confidentiality
- Use company-approved VPN at all times
- Keep work-related files on company servers only
- Do not share access credentials with anyone
- Report any security incidents immediately

## Performance Expectations
- Same performance standards apply for WFH as in-office work
- Regular status updates with manager are required
- Attendance in scheduled meetings is mandatory
- Response time to emails should be within 4 business hours

## Termination of WFH Privilege
WFH privileges can be revoked if:
- Consistent poor performance
- Missed deadlines or meetings
- Data security violations
- Lack of availability during core hours
